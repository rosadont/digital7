Escape the lab!

  • High Concept: Get the lab experiment out of the lab by collecting potions to become human.
  • Player Experience Goals: I hope they don't get too bored with my game. Don't expect too much and just have fun with it.
  • Genre: This game fits into a kids genre, because it's colorful and cartoonish for children.
  • Game play:
  • 1. Wait for everything to load.
  • 2. Get monster to collect potions.
  • 3. Avoid obstacles by pressing on the cursor.
  • 4. Left arrow = move left
  • 5. Right arrow = move right
  • 5. Up arrow = jump
  • 6. Avoid the quicksand because you are not able to escape out of it.
  • 7. Avoid your monster clones that are chasing you.
  • Prototype goal: Reach the maximum percentage and escape the lab.
  • Features: Cool music and very colorful images.
  • Setting/Premise:
    • The player just escaped the lab, just to encounter that the lab is within another environment. So the player has to escape the labyrinth before he/she is able to reach the outside world. Also, for the exit to show itself, the player must be fully human to pass through.
  • Story: The player was experimented on and is no longer human. He/She is now green and not recognizable. The player must find a way to change back into a human and escape this nightmare.
  • Target Audience: This game is targeted for children 5 years old and under.
  • Play time: The game is finished in like 30 - 45 seconds if you win. The game ends quickly when caught, which is most of the time.
  • Strategies: The player should first try to avoid the obstacles. Be quick about it and reach the percentage. You'll die if you touch them.
